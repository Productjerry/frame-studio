Put your logo here.

1. Save your logo image in this folder named exactly:  logo.png
   (A square image works best — e.g. 256×256 or larger. PNG with a
   transparent or white background looks cleanest in the round badge.)

2. Commit / upload it to GitHub the same way you uploaded the project.
   Vercel redeploys automatically and your logo replaces the "Rc" mark
   everywhere (admin sidebar, user header, login screen).

If logo.png is missing, the app shows a simple "Rc" lettermark instead,
so nothing breaks.

Prefer an SVG or a different filename? Open src/components/ui.jsx, find
LogoMark, and change the src="/logo.png" line to match.
